STAY SAFE ONLINE - PREVENTION CHECKLIST
For College Students in India

GENERAL SAFETY:
1. Use strong passwords with a mix of letters, numbers 
   and symbols - never use your name or birthdate
2. Enable two-factor authentication on all accounts 
   including email, Instagram and LinkedIn
3. Never click on links received from unknown numbers 
   on WhatsApp or SMS
4. Log out of all accounts when using shared or 
   public computers
5. Regularly check your bank and UPI statements for 
   unknown transactions
6. Do not share personal information like Aadhaar, 
   PAN or college ID on unverified websites

UPI AND FINANCIAL SAFETY:
7. Never share your UPI PIN with anyone - not even 
   someone claiming to be from your bank
8. Remember - entering your PIN on a collect request 
   means you are SENDING money, not receiving it
   
REPORTING:
- National Cyber Crime Portal: cybercrime.gov.in
- Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930 (available 24x7)
- Report immediately if you are a victim - 
  faster reporting increases chance of recovery
