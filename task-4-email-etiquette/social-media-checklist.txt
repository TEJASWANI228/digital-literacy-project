SOCIAL MEDIA DO'S AND DON'TS FOR COLLEGE STUDENTS

DO'S:
1. Keep your profiles professional and up to date
2. Think before you post - ask yourself if your professor 
   would be okay seeing it
3. Use privacy settings to control who sees your posts
4. Give credit when sharing someone else's content
5. Use social media to build your professional network 
   on LinkedIn and GitHub

DON'TS:
1. Don't share your location, phone number, or home 
   address publicly
2. Don't post during exams or college hours
3. Don't spread unverified news or rumors
4. Don't accept friend requests from people you don't know
5. Don't post anything disrespectful about your 
   institution or teachers
